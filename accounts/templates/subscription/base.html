Frequently Asked Questions

We offer the lowest convenience fee in India at a flat 5% on every ticket booking. This ensures a seamless experience with no hidden charges!

What is the ₹555 Theatre Pass?

The ₹555 Theatre Pass offers you access to 5 movies per month for ₹555. You can book one ticket per day, with a maximum value of ₹200 per ticket.

How does the referral program work?

For successful 5 referrals, you will earn rewards. You can refer friends using your unique referral link, and once they sign up, you will receive benefits like discounts or bonus tickets.

Can I use the pass to watch any movie?

Yes, you can use the ₹555 Theatre Pass to watch any movie, subject to availability and a maximum ticket price of ₹200 per day. Any amount exceeding ₹200 will be paid from your end.

Can I watch movies on weekends?

Standard users can book tickets for movies only from Monday to Thursday. However, VIP Club members can book tickets any day of the week.

Is there a limit to the number of movies I can watch in a month?

Yes, with the ₹555 Theatre Pass, you can watch up to 5 movies per month, one per day.

What happens if I don't use all my 5 movie credits in a month?

If you don't use all 5 credits, they will not carry over to the next month. The pass is valid only for the current month's movies.

How can I make payments?

You can make payments securely using various payment methods such as credit/debit cards, net banking, or digital wallets.

How do I get my tickets?

Once you book your ticket via the website, you will receive an electronic ticket (QR code) via email, which you can scan at the theatre for entry.

Can I upgrade my plan to a VIP Club membership?

Yes, you can upgrade your ₹555 Theatre Pass to a VIP Club membership by paying an additional fee. VIP members have the privilege of booking tickets any day of the week.

What should I do if I face issues with my ticket booking?

If you encounter any issues, please reach out to our customer support team at support@hi5.com, and we'll assist you promptly.

Is there an auto-renewal option for the ₹555 pass?

Yes, your subscription will automatically renew at ₹555 every month unless you choose to cancel before the renewal date.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time before the next billing cycle. However, no refunds will be issued for the current month.

How can I change my subscription details or update my account information?

You can update your account details by logging into your profile on our website and making the necessary changes in the "Account Settings" section.

What do I do if I don't receive my confirmation email?

Please check your spam/junk folder. If you still haven't received it, contact our support team, and we'll help you resolve the issue.
